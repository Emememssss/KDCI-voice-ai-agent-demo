// Demo Instructions

How to Try This

Two ways to interact — choose based on what you want to experience.

📞 Via Phone Call

Full end-to-end experience including live human transfer.

  1. 01 — Dial +1 681 293 1947 (hold 0 for + on your mobile)
  2. 02 — Elaine (AI) answers. Ask 2–3 questions from the list below.
  3. 03 — Say "I want to talk to a human" or "connect me to a person"
  4. 04 — Wait — the call transfers automatically to a live KDCI agent
  5. 05 — You're now speaking directly with a human representative

Note: International call rates apply from Philippines (~₱15–25/min via Smart/Globe). Use WiFi calling or Google Voice to reduce cost.

🌐 Via Browser Widget

Quick demo of AI conversation — no phone needed.

  1. 01 — Click "Start a call" in the widget above
  2. 02 — Allow microphone access when prompted
  3. 03 — Speak naturally — ask Elaine questions
  4. 04 — Say "I want to talk to a human" to see escalation behavior
  5. 05 — Note: transfer to live agent is not available in browser mode

Limitation: Browser widget cannot forward to a live phone agent. This is an ElevenLabs platform constraint for web-based sessions. Full transfer only works via phone call.

// Suggested Questions

What to Ask Elaine

✅ General — from knowledge base
  • "What services does KDCI offer?"
  • "Where is KDCI located?"
  • "How long has KDCI been operating?"
💰 Pricing — from knowledge base
  • "How much does it cost to hire a virtual assistant?"
  • "What are the rates for a customer service representative?"
  • "Can you give me pricing for a graphic designer?"
  • "Are there any hidden fees?"
⚙️ Process — from knowledge base
  • "How long does it take to get someone started?"
  • "What's the recruitment process like?"
  • "Do we get to interview candidates?"
  • "What tools do your teams use for communication?"
🏢 Department-specific — from knowledge base
  • "Can you handle Product Information Management?"
  • "What eCommerce platforms does your PIM team support?"
  • "How does quality assurance work for creative deliverables?"
🌐 Multilingual — try a different language
  • "Pwede bang mag-inquire tungkol sa outsourcing services ninyo?" (Filipino)
  • "Hola, me gustaría saber sobre sus servicios." (Spanish)
⚠️ Honest escalation — demonstrates AI boundaries
  • "What is the exact monthly pricing for a 5-person team?"
  • "Can you pull up my account details?"
  • "What's the status of my current project?"

Expected behavior: Elaine will encourage a consultation or offer to have a specialist follow up — demonstrating proper AI boundaries and honest escalation.

🔴 Escalation trigger — human transfer
  • "I want to talk to a human."
  • "Connect me to a real person."
  • "I'm not satisfied — transfer me."
